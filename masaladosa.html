Masala Dosa

Tamil Nadu

Masala Dosa is a classic South Indian dish that has won hearts across the globe. This thin, crispy crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter is filled with a spiced potato mixture and served with coconut chutney and sambar. Originating from Tamil Nadu, the masala dosa showcases the perfect balance of textures and flavors - the crispy golden exterior complemented by the soft, flavorful potato filling. A staple breakfast item in South India, this dish has become synonymous with Indian cuisine worldwide.
